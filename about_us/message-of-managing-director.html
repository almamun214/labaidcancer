SAKIF SHAMIM

MANAGING DIRECTOR, Labaid Cancer Hospital And Super Speciality Centre

Labaid Cancer Hospital and Super Speciality Centre, Dhaka, Bangladesh is the first ever comprehensive cancer care facility in the country. It is established with the vision to provide healthcare services with a spirit of patient centricity and ethical practice. With the right harmony of 30 years of trust and legacy of Labaid and modern leadership in healthcare, this hospital is aiming to revolutionize the overall cancer treatment and care of Bangladesh.

We are committed to be the true partners for health needs for the community, ensuring that along with guidance, attention and care, expertise is delivered with cutting edge technology and precision.

Labaid Cancer Hospital and Super Specialty Center also aims to be the pioneer in changing the perception that, quality care for cancer patients provided by private hospitals is beyond the reach of common people. With choice of cost-effective care and a wide range of services and facilities to choose from, we are contributing to the overall development of health sector of our country.
