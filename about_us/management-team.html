Dr. George Noel Fernandes

Chief Operating Officer

Dr. George Noel Fernandes has been appointed as Chief Operating Officer in Labaid Cancer Hospital and Super Specialty Centre. Dr. George Noel Fernandes holds a MBBS degree from St. John's Medical College; Bangalore University, Post Graduate Diploma in Preventive and Promotive Healthcare from Apollo Hospital Education and Research Foundation; Hyderabad and Diploma in Travel Medicine from Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Glasgow; Glasgow. He has over 25 Years of healthcare experience in Asia including China and Japan, Europe, Africa, Middle- East and Americas.

Has worked with variety of organizations such as tertiary care hospitals, multinational corporations & insurance & medical assistance companies to deliver best available healthcare practices with keen focus on business growth and quality.

Prior to joining Labaid Cancer Hospital and Super Specialty Center, Dr. George Noel Fernandes worked in Saudi German Hospital, Dubai UAE as Chief Operating Officer. Throughout his career, he also worked with some reputed hospitals and international organization i.e. Datta Meghe Group of Hospitals as Chief Operating Officer and Chief Growth Officer; BM Birla Hospital as Vice President and Head of Marketing; Apollo Hospitals as Vice President and Medical Director; Emirate Airline Group as Group Head-Medical Benefits. Has worked as the Regional Medical director for Europe Assistance and Medical Assistance companies such as Europ Assistance IHS & international SOS.

He is the thought leader in implementing and planning air medical evacuation & disaster management. Has been personally involved in more than 500 medical air evacuations and has been on ground in more than 45 disasters across the globe. His expertise is in healthcare funding; Building, Commissioning and operating and managing Corporate and Medical Colleges; Medical evacuations; Disaster Management; Organ Transplant; E-Health, building Virtual Hospitals and in AI Healthcare.
